This document is an official proposal (public offer) of LLP "Imbir Group", hereinafter referred to as the "Seller", and contains all the essential terms for the provision of services and the purchase of Gift Certificates at the Thai massage and SPA salons "Imbir Thai Spa Classic" (hereinafter — the Salon/Contractor), information about which is posted on the official Website www.imbir.kz and/or its sections and subdomains, including www.sert.imbir.kz.

In accordance with Article 395 of the Civil Code of the RK, a proposal containing all the essential terms of a contract, from which the intention of the person making the proposal to conclude a contract on the terms specified in the proposal with anyone who responds is apparent, is recognized as an offer (a public offer). The public contract is deemed concluded from the moment of acceptance of this offer by virtue of Article 396 of the Civil Code of the RK and is valid until the parties have fulfilled all their obligations under the Public Contract.

From the moment of acceptance of this offer, the person (natural or legal) performing the acceptance becomes the Buyer.

Acceptance of this Public Offer means that the Public Contract has been concluded — on the terms specified in this Public Offer.

Acceptance, i.e. the full and unconditional acceptance of this public offer, is performed by one of the following means:

- clicking the button "I confirm that I have read the terms of the public offer, the privacy and personal data processing policy and the rules for the purchase, use and refund of certificates, fully accept them and give my consent to enter into the public contract", located on the Website www.imbir.kz and/or its sections and subdomains, including www.sert.imbir.kz;

- making partial (advance) or full payment for the provision of services;

- purchasing a Gift Certificate and paying for it in full;

- using a Gift Certificate for the provision of services;

- booking a service by any means and providing accurate personal data, including completing the Client Questionnaire.

The performance by the Buyer (or the Holder of the Gift Certificate) of any of the above actions constitutes their unconditional agreement with the terms of the Public Contract and their accession to it as a whole.

Appendix No. 1 to the Public Contract

Contraindications to receiving the Services:

  1. Pregnancy at any stage, the lactation period
  2. Allergic reactions
  3. Recently undergone operations (less than a year ago)
  4. Spinal problems (hernias, protrusions), fractures
  5. Osteoporosis
  6. Medical contraindications to massage
  7. Cardiovascular diseases
  8. Acute heart failure
  9. High blood pressure (hypertension)
  10. Epilepsy
  11. HIV, hepatitis
  12. Oncological diseases
  13. All chronic diseases in the acute stage
  14. Fever
  15. Feverish condition
  16. The period after abortions or childbirth (within 60 days)
  17. Acute pain
  18. Neuralgia
  19. Inflammatory reactions in the body
  20. The rehabilitation period after surgery, radiation therapy or chemotherapy
  21. Arterial diseases
  22. Predisposition to the formation of blood clots
  23. A state of intoxication (hangover syndrome, poisoning)
  24. Tuberculosis
  25. Renal failure
  26. Blood diseases, bleeding of various etiologies and a predisposition to them
  27. Atherosclerosis of the peripheral vessels
  28. Varicose veins
  29. Delicate massage in the area of raised moles and papillomas
  30. Dermatological diseases (eczema, dermatitis, psoriasis, shingles) — the affected area should not be massaged
  31. Prostheses — implants (e.g. breast, hip or knee joint) — the affected area should not be massaged
